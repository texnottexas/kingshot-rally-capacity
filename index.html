II. The Math

How the rally screen adds up

Recommended vs Safe. Recommended uses the leaders' average pool: a number that works well across the group on most calls. Safe uses the smallest leader's pool: a slightly lower number that guarantees all 14 joiners fit no matter who ends up calling. With a single leader (or matched capacities) the two values are identical.

Why cap at 14 joiners? A rally only has 14 joiner slots. Beyond fourteen the math stops changing - the first fourteen to tap fill the rally, and any extra allies start the next call.
