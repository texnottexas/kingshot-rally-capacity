# Bear Hunt Rally Capacity - A Kingshot Coordination Codex

A single-page calculator and visual reference for **Kingshot** bear-hunt rallies:
where the in-game numbers come from (Command Center, March Setup, Rally Screen),
how they combine into the rally-screen total, and what each joiner should march
so all 14 joiner slots fill perfectly.

**Live page:** https://texnottexas.github.io/kingshot-rally-capacity/

## Why it exists

Bear hunt coordination usually breaks down on the math: people guess at march sizes,
slots end up under- or over-committed, and the rally never quite uses its full
capacity. This tool turns the three on-screen numbers into a single per-joiner
recommendation and shows how the math behaves at every common turnout (3 players
through 20+).

## Structure

- `index.html` - the entire self-contained page (styles + calculator inline)
- `assets/` - the three annotated game screenshots (Command Center, March Setup, Rally Screen)
